## Identified Navigation Anomalies

1. **Header Link Duplication:** The top navigation bar items are repeated exactly on the persistent sidebars of 42 pages, creating duplicate interactive entry points.
2. **Dead Ends:** Hashtag links ('#') have been replaced with active routes, but pages like `/sovereign-fund-investment-portfolio` still lack internal links back to the main portal.
3. **Contextual Competing Navigation:** The floating Portal Directory overlay and the top header navbar both link to `/serve-abia` and `/secure-abia`, offering competing options.
