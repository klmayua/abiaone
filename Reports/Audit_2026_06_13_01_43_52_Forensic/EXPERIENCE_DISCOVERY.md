# Experience Discovery

Derives experiences directly from actual page content rather than routes.

## Observed Experience Clusters

### 1. The Heritage & Travel Hub (`discover_abia`, `visit_abia`)
- **Evidence-backed pages:** `/tourism-culture`, `/digital-archives-heritage`, `/state-archives`
- **Observed Headings:** "Digital Archives & Heritage", "Tourism & Culture"
- **Observed Content:** Interactive history logs, historical timelines, and local maps.
- **Primary User Persona:** Tourists, researchers, diaspora.

### 2. The Commerce & Investment Portal (`invest_abia`)
- **Evidence-backed pages:** `/invest-in-aba`, `/invest-abia`, `/commerce-trade`
- **Observed Headings:** "Global Investment Gateway", "Aba Industrial Marketplace"
- **Observed Content:** Trade listings, economic stats, and investment registration forms.
- **Primary User Persona:** Investors, business developers, exporters.

### 3. The Citizen Services Hub (`serve_abia`, `work_abia`)
- **Evidence-backed pages:** `/serve-abia`, `/gada`, `/procurement-tenders-registry`, `/civil-service-portal`
- **Observed Headings:** "Digital Government Services", "GADA Citizen Workspace"
- **Observed Content:** Tender registries, business license forms, and service fee tables.
- **Primary User Persona:** Citizens, contractors, job-seekers.
