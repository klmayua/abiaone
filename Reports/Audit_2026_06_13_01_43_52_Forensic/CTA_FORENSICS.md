## Conversion Strategy Review

- **Primary Conversion Goal:** Direct citizen input via forms (e.g. incident reports, investment registry submissions).
- **Secondary Conversion Goal:** Information consumption (e.g. downloading agricultural manuals or viewing budget telemetry).
- **Competing Conversions:** Many pages feature multiple primary green buttons (e.g. `/serve-abia` has over 40 buttons of similar visual weight), which dilutes user focus.
