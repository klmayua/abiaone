# Final Platform Governance Model

## Governance Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

1. **Page Creation SOP:** No new route folders may be added without matching entry in the canonical Experience Registry.
2. **Responsive Layout SOP:** Standard viewports must use Tailwind container breakpoints (`md:`, `lg:`) instead of separate route mockups.
3. **Sidebar SOP:** Sidebars are restricted to dashboard/workspace layouts.
