## 1. MINISTRY OWNERSHIP METHODOLOGY

Ministry ownership is determined by cross-referencing:
- `ministry_relationship` field from prior PAGE_OWNERSHIP_REGISTRY.md (69 pages with explicit ministry assignments)
- `Ministry Owner` field from SERVICE_REGISTRY.md (5 core services)
- `Owner` field from CAPABILITY_REGISTRY.md (20 capabilities)
- Page route and title analysis from PAGE_CONTENT_FORENSICS.md
- No assumptions made — every assignment requires evidence

## 6. EVIDENCE-BASED MINISTRY RECOMMENDATIONS

### 6.1 Ministry of Digital Economy & Transformation — Over-Attribution Flag
- **Evidence:** 38 pages assigned but only 2 services + 3 capabilities
- **Root Cause:** Prior audit defaulted many pages to "Ministry of Digital Government" when actual ministry ownership was unclear
- **Impact:** Inflates MDET page count; understates other ministries' actual ownership
- **Recommendation:** Re-audit all 38 MDET pages; re-assign pages to actual domain ministries (e.g., `/health-wellness` → MOH, `/agri-transformation` → MOA)

### 6.2 Ministry of Health — Variant Consolidation
- **Evidence:** 7 pages = 1 service (Health Service)
- **Impact:** `/health-wellness`, `/health-wellness-2`, `/health-wellness-mobile` are 3 variants of same page; `/ministry-of-health`, `/ministry-of-health-2`, `/ministry-of-health-mobile`, `/ministry-of-health-mobile-2` are 4 variants of same page
- **Recommendation:** Consolidate to 2 canonical pages: `/health-wellness` (public info) + `/ministry-of-health` (ministry portal)

### 6.3 Ministry of Agriculture — Variant Consolidation
- **Evidence:** 6 pages = 1 service (Agriculture Service)
- **Impact:** `/ministry-of-agriculture`, `/ministry-of-agriculture-2`, `/ministry-of-agriculture-3`, `/ministry-of-agriculture-mobile`, `/ministry-of-agriculture-mobile-2` are 5 variants of same page
- **Recommendation:** Consolidate to 2 canonical pages: `/agri-transformation` (public dashboard) + `/ministry-of-agriculture` (ministry portal)

### 6.4 Ministry of Security — Variant Consolidation
- **Evidence:** 7 pages = 1 service (Public Reporting System)
- **Impact:** `/secure-abia`, `/secure-abia-2`, `/secure-abia-3`, `/secure-abia-mobile`, `/secure-abia-mobile-2` are 5 variants of same page
- **Recommendation:** Consolidate to 1 canonical page: `/secure-abia` with responsive design
