## 5. EXPERIENCE GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLES

### 5.1 One Experience, One Service Owner
- Every experience has exactly 1 primary service owner
- Secondary services are support-only, not operationally responsible

### 5.2 One Experience, One Ministry Owner
- Every experience has exactly 1 ministry owner
- Ministry is accountable for experience quality and content accuracy

### 5.3 Experience Page Minimum
- Every experience has at least 1 canonical page
- No experience has more than 3 canonical pages (target)

### 5.4 Experience CTA Consistency
- Every experience has exactly 1 primary conversion point
- CTA text is consistent across all experience pages
