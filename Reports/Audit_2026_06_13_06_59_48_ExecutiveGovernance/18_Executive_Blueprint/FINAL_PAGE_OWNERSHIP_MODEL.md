## 5. OWNERSHIP GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLES

### 5.1 One Page, One Owner
- Every canonical page has exactly 1 ministry owner
- Ministry is accountable for page content, accuracy, and quality

### 5.2 Ownership by Domain
- Ownership is assigned by domain expertise, not file location
- Ministry of Health owns health pages, not Ministry of Digital Economy & Transformation

### 5.3 Agency Delegation
- Ministries may delegate operational ownership to agencies
- Agency operates the service; ministry provides oversight

### 5.4 Ownership Transfer
- Ownership transfers require approval from both current and new owner
- Platform Team updates ownership registry after transfer
- Navigation and CTAs are updated to reflect new ownership

---

## 6. OWNERSHIP COMPLIANCE

### 6.1 Compliance Rules
- Every canonical page must have exactly 1 ministry owner
- Ministry owner must be a valid ministry in the registry
- Agency owner must be a valid agency in the registry
- Ownership must align with page domain (health pages → Ministry of Health)

### 6.2 Compliance Monitoring
- Monthly ownership audit (verify all pages have owners)
- Quarterly domain alignment audit (verify ownership matches page domain)
- Annual ownership review (verify ownership still appropriate)

### 6.3 Compliance Violations
| Violation | Severity | Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Missing page owner | HIGH | Assign owner within 48 hours |
| Incorrect domain alignment | MEDIUM | Reassign within 1 week |
| Missing agency delegation | LOW | Document delegation within 2 weeks |
