# Final Platform Governance Model

## Governance Standard Operating Procedures

1. **Page Naming nomenclature:** Every route page must have a unique, lowercase name matching its purpose. Suffix routes should be avoided in production.
2. **Sidebar Rules:** sidebars are prohibited on public pages and allowed only for dashboards/workspace portals.
3. **CTA Priorities:** Every page must have exactly one primary CTA (green button) and no more than two secondary actions.
4. **Layout Parity:** Style overrides must continue to reside in local style tags via `dangerouslySetInnerHTML` to prevent global compilation shifts.
